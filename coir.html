Geo Textile

Geo Textiles can be woven using both automated machines & hand operated machines. Geo Textiles which are made by machines use only machine spun twine, however hand operated machines can handle both machine spun twine and hand made yarn. The product could be either rolled up or baled depending on the requirement.

Standard geo textiles come in 400g/sqm, 700g/sqm and 900g/sqm. Nevertheless we can supply between 300g/sqm to 100g/sqm based on the customer requirement. The weight per square meter is controlled by changing the type of twine used and number of twines used.

Advantages of using Geo Textiles;

  • As a temporary soil covering until re-vegetation takes place
  • As a shield against wind or water erosion.
  • To re-enforce the soil
  • For various uses at worksites
  • Road construction uses
  • As a protective measure at river banks
  • As a stopper for composed material, seeds and seed links
  • High tensile strength
  • 100% environmentally friendly, bio-degradable product.
  • Various sizes are available

Coir Logs / Geo Logs / Coir Wattle / Coir Rolls

Coir Logs are strong and flexible. These are compact coir cores which form a Coir Web for superior filtration, covered by exterior coir mesh netting. Coir logs aid in the stabilization and revegetation of sites where you encounter steep slopes or exposure to waves along or currents which cause instability on a site. The Coir Logs are designed to withstand the wave impact and storm water sheetflow forces without deformation or loss of material ensuring maximum erosion prevention. Coir Logs enhance plant growth and microbial activity. In flowing water areas, the coir logs are placed directly on the bank, often retaining fill material or securing new channel alignment. In standing water, the Coir Logs are placed offshore to break waves and encourage sedimentation and re-vegetation. Coir Logs collect and hold mineral and organic particles, provide a stable substrate which promotes root growth, and slowly biodegrade to leave a self maintaining erosion prevention system.

Types of Coir logs

  • Needle Punched Coir Roll
  • Square Net Type Coir Rolls
  • Square Net Type Holed Square Rolls
  • Fishing Net Type Coir Rolls
  • Fishing net type holed coir rolls with Coir twine and or synthetic rope

Applications

  • Channel stabilization
  • Shoreline stabilization
  • River bank stabilization
  • Wetland restoration
  • Wildlife habitat
  • Landscaping

Advantages

  • Made from 100% natural coconut fiber
  • Excellent planting medium
  • Sediment control structure and encourages deposition
  • Economical
  • Safe to wildlife

Coir Log/ Coir wattle diameters and lengths vary according to customer requirements

Coir Twisted Fiber

Coir fiber is elastic enough to twist without breaking and it holds a curl as though permanently waved. Machine twisted fiber is produced by blending bristle and mattress fibers in varying ratios. They are then spun and curled into a thick single-ply rope. Machine twisted fiber is also known as curled fiber. Grow Poles

100% environmental friendly, bio- degradable coconut fibers are used for manufacture this ideal plant support and growing aid product. Grow poles have a high water holding capacity and this ensures the plant to grow stronger.

This product could be used in any clay or plastic pot, in plant Nurseries / horticultural farms or as a decorative item in domestic environments as well. Grow pole is a supporting pole for potted plant creepers to grow both indoors and outdoors. Our grow poles are made by wrapping natural coir fiber in either forms of mattress fiber rubberized coir pads or coir yarn around a hollow PVC pipe. The high moisture retention capacity of the stick enables the roots of the plant to grip natural organic mattress of coir fiber.

We offer this product in three different types;

  • GROW POLE -coir twine tightly wound round the pole
  • COIR POLE - with natural coir wound on the pole or thinner coir yarn wrapped to hold tightly
  • COCO POLE - coir yarn tightly wound round the pole

The product is available in various diameters and lengths according to customer requirements.

Coir Gabions

Coir fiber stuffed and encased in biodegradable netting, formed in a square shape structure. Coco gabion can be made with or without a steel frame. This is an ideal product to be used in at a base of a slope where a low toe wall is needed to stabilize the slope and reduce slope steepness in a stream bank. Appropriate for use with soil bioengineering systems and vegetative plantings to stabilize the upper bank and secure a regenerative source of stream bank vegetation.

The main advantage of coco gabion is that it can be planted with live cuttings on any of its surface area. Fiber substrate filled inside the coco gabion is an ideal medium for root development for the cuttings and, subsequently, these plants will consolidate the structure and bind it to the slope.

Specifications:

  • Size: 100 x 100 x 50 cm
  • Weight: 20Kg

Gabion size can vary according to the buyer's specifications. Also available: coco gabion filled with coir fiber/chopped coir fiber and encased in a rectangular basket shape with galvanized wired steel mesh.
